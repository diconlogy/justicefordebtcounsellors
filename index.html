About

We are a small group of debt counsellors representing both small and large debt counselling firms that have decided to take on what we believe is an unfair framework enforced by the National Credit Regulator (NCR) towards registered and abiding debt counsellors. They have made it in many cases impossible for debt counsellors to run profitable practises and have over the years imposed and enforced varies fines and penalties on debt counsellors which we believe is not in the best interest of the debt counsellor or the industry. If no one fights for change and a fair playing field, then the industry will not be sustainable.

Background

JusticeForDebtCounsellors.org is exactly what we stand for, justice and fairness for debt counsellors in South Africa, there needs to be a voice for this part of the industry that is not afraid to legally challenge the National Credit Regulator (NCR) and fight for the rights of debt counsellors who practice in a framework set out by the NCR that we believe have made it impossible for debt counsellors to run profitable practices, service their clients to the best of their ability and still maintain good relationships with industry stakeholders.

Mission

As you can imagine, we are taking on a goliath, as the NCR holds the mandate to regulate the industry and have the budget of government to back them. This have led to an environment where debt counsellors who do not have the financial backing have gone under or left the industry where the NCR have made it impossible for them to operate in and even imposed fines on such debt counsellors.
